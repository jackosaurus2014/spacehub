# Space Tycoon: Alternative Mid & Endgame Progression Design

> **Goal**: Replace prestige (reset-based progression) with systems that compound achievement without losing progress. Give players meaningful content from mid-game ($10B+) through infinite endgame.

---

## Current State Analysis

- **35 buildings** across 5 tiers ($15M - $200B)
- **~230 research items** across 17 categories and 5 tiers
- **25 locations** (11 base + 14 colony, total unlock cost ~$2.3T)
- **44 services** generating up to ~$1.9B/month total
- **17 contracts**, **26 achievements**, **10 milestones**
- A dedicated player hits the content wall at **3-6 weeks** real-time

## System 1: Personal Megastructures

Massive personal construction projects (not alliance-shared) that take days-weeks and provide permanent empire bonuses.

| Megastructure | Total Cost | Build Time | Key Bonus |
|--------------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Space Elevator | $160B | 6 days | -75% launch costs, +50% launch revenue |
| Dyson Swarm | $225B | 7 days | +25% energy revenue, -20% maintenance |
| Orbital Ring | $450B | 9 days | -50% Earth-orbit costs, +2 build slots |
| Matrioshka Brain | $1T | 12 days | +50% research speed, unlocks Infinite Research |
| Generation Ship | $750B | 14 days | $500M/mo passive, opens Interstellar Operations |
| Stellar Engine | $2T | 21 days | +100% all mining output |

**Unlocks at**: $100B net worth + 50 research + 8 locations.
**Max 2 under construction simultaneously.**

## System 2: Corporation Tiers / Company Evolution

Your company evolves through named tiers, each unlocking new capabilities:

| Tier | Name | Trigger | Key Unlock |
|------|------|---------|-----------|
| 1 | Startup | Game start | 1 research slot, 3 build slots |
| 2 | Enterprise | $1B earned + 5 bldgs + 5 research | 5 build slots, Workforce tab |
| 3 | Corporation | $10B + 15/15 + 5 locations | 2 research slots, Subsidiaries, Market limit orders |
| 4 | Conglomerate | $100B + 30/30 + 8 locations | Specialization Trees, +10% revenue |
| 5 | Megacorp | $500B + 50/50 + all base locations | Megastructures, 3 research slots, 10 build slots |
| 6 | Interstellar Consortium | $2T + all research + megastructure + 5 colonies | Victory Conditions, unlimited slots, +25% revenue |

Each tier grants a one-time cash bonus and a visible badge on leaderboards.

## System 7: Legacy System (Prestige Alternative)

Every major milestone permanently improves your empire. **You never lose anything.**

**Tier 1 — Foundation** (early game):
- First $1B earned → +2% revenue permanently
- 10 buildings → -2% maintenance permanently
- 5 research → +2% research speed permanently

**Tier 2 — Expansion** (mid game):
- Reach Mars → +3% revenue
- $10B earned → +3% revenue
- 25 buildings → -3% maintenance

**Tier 3 — Dominance** (late game):
- Reach Jupiter → +5% revenue
- $100B earned → +5% revenue
- All base locations → -5% unlock costs

**Tier 4 — Transcendence** (endgame):
- $1T earned → +10% revenue
- Megastructure complete → -10% build costs
- Victory condition → +5% everything

**Stretch Legacies (infinite):** Every additional $1T → +1% revenue, every 10 buildings past 100 → -0.5% maintenance.

Total compounded bonuses comparable to 3-4 prestige cycles, but nothing is ever lost.

## System 8: Infinite Scaling

### 8A: Building Upgrades (No Cap)
Remove the level 2 cap. Each additional level: +10% revenue, -2% maintenance. Cost 1.5x per level.

### 8B: Infinite Research
After Matrioshka Brain, repeatable research: "Revenue Optimization N" (+1% revenue, cost $10B × 1.5^N).

### 8C: Fleet Scaling
Ship upgrades with no cap. Fleet milestones: 10/20/50/100/500 ships grant fleet-wide bonuses.

### 8D: Colony Population
Population grows 1% monthly (compounding). Each 1,000 pop → +1% location revenue. No cap.

### 8E: Reputation System
Global score from all positive actions. Every 100 rep → +1% contract rewards, every 500 → +1% revenue.
