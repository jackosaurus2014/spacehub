## 1. Content Strategy Overview

### Target Audience

| Segment | Title Examples | Pain Points | Content Hook |
|---------|---------------|-------------|--------------|
| Space Industry Executives | VP Strategy, CEO, COO | Fragmented data, slow market intel | Cost savings, competitive edge |
| Engineers & Technical Staff | Systems Engineer, Mission Planner | Manual research, siloed tools | Real-time data, 3D visualization |
| Investors & Analysts | VC Partner, Equity Analyst | Opaque market data, high research costs | Market intelligence, valuations |
| Policy Makers & Regulators | Spectrum Manager, Compliance Officer | Regulatory complexity, treaty tracking | Compliance tools, regulatory alerts |
| Entrepreneurs & Founders | Startup CEO, Business Development | Market entry barriers, expensive tools | Affordable pricing, free tier |

### Content Pillars

| Pillar | Share | Purpose | Example Topics |
|--------|-------|---------|----------------|
| **Thought Leadership** | 40% | Establish authority, drive organic reach | Industry trends, market analysis, future predictions |
| **Product Showcases** | 25% | Demonstrate value, drive sign-ups | Feature demos, use cases, comparison posts |
| **Industry Insights** | 20% | Provide data-driven value, build trust | Launch stats, funding data, regulatory updates |
| **Engagement** | 15% | Build community, boost algorithm reach | Polls, trivia, career advice, questions |

### Posting Schedule

- **Frequency**: 1 post per day (30 posts over 30 days)
- **Optimal Times**: Tuesday through Thursday, 8:00--10:00 AM ET (peak B2B engagement window)
- **Secondary Windows**: Monday and Friday, 9:00--11:00 AM ET
- **Weekend Posts**: Saturday/Sunday, 10:00 AM--12:00 PM ET (lighter, engagement-focused)

### Brand Voice Guidelines

- **Tone**: Authoritative yet accessible. Data-driven but not dry.
- **Perspective**: Industry insider who democratizes access to information.
- **Differentiator**: We make space industry intelligence affordable. Competitors charge $3,000--$15,000/yr. SpaceNexus starts free.
- **Avoid**: Hype without substance, vague claims, overly promotional language without value.
- **Include**: Specific numbers, data points, real industry context, actionable takeaways.

### Key Metrics to Track

- **Impressions**: Target 5,000+ per post within 30 days
- **Engagement Rate**: Target 3--5% (LinkedIn average is 2%)
- **Click-Through Rate**: Target 1.5--2.5% on CTA links
- **Follower Growth**: Target 15--20% month-over-month
- **Article Views**: Target 500+ per long-form article

### LinkedIn Algorithm Best Practices (2026)

1. **First 90 Minutes Are Critical**: LinkedIn's algorithm evaluates engagement in the first 90 minutes to decide distribution. Post when your audience is most active (8-10 AM ET for B2B space).

2. **Dwell Time Matters**: Longer posts that keep readers scrolling get higher distribution. Use line breaks, numbered lists, and "hook" opening lines to increase dwell time.

3. **Comments > Reactions**: One comment is worth approximately 10 reactions in LinkedIn's algorithm. End every post with a question to drive comments.

4. **Respond to Every Comment**: Reply to comments within 2 hours. Each reply creates a new "engagement signal" that boosts distribution.

5. **No External Links in Post Body**: LinkedIn deprioritizes posts with external links (they want users to stay on LinkedIn). Put the link in the FIRST COMMENT instead, and reference it in the post with "Link in comments."

6. **Document/Carousel Posts Outperform**: PDF carousels get 2-3x the engagement of image posts. They increase dwell time as users swipe through slides.

7. **Video Under 90 Seconds**: Native video outperforms YouTube links. Keep under 90 seconds. Add captions (85% of LinkedIn video is watched without sound).

8. **Posting Frequency**: 1 post per day is optimal. More than 2 per day dilutes engagement. If you have extra content, save it or put it in comments.

9. **Engage Before and After Posting**: Spend 15 minutes engaging with other posts before and after your post goes live. This signals to LinkedIn that your account is "active" and boosts distribution.

10. **Avoid Pods and Engagement Bait**: LinkedIn actively penalizes "engagement pods" (groups that artificially like/comment on each others' posts) and engagement bait ("Like if you agree").
