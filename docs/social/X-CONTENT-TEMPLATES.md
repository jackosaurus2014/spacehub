# SpaceNexus X/Twitter Content Templates

10 ready-to-post templates for the @spacenexus account. Replace `{{VARIABLES}}` with current data before posting.

---

## 1. Launch Countdown Tweet

**Template:**
```
T-{{HOURS}} hours until {{MISSION}} launches from {{SITE}}

{{VEHICLE}} will carry {{PAYLOAD}} to {{ORBIT}}

Track it live on SpaceNexus:
spacenexus.us/launch-manifest

#SpaceLaunch #{{MISSION_HASHTAG}} #SpaceIndustry
```

**Optimal posting time:** 6-12 hours before launch window opens
**Hashtag suggestions:** #SpaceLaunch #Rocket #SpaceX #RocketLab #ULA #LaunchDay #Space

## General Posting Guidelines

### Optimal Posting Schedule
| Day | Time (ET) | Content Type |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| Monday | 8-10 AM | Industry stat, market data |
| Tuesday | 10 AM-12 PM | New article, feature announcement |
| Wednesday | 12-2 PM | Engagement question, "Did you know" |
| Thursday | 9-11 AM | Article promotion, conference coverage |
| Friday | 2-4 PM | Weekly digest, satellite milestone |
| Saturday | 10 AM-12 PM | Space fact, engagement question |

### Core Hashtags (always include 1-2)
- `#SpaceIndustry`
- `#SpaceNexus`
- `#SpaceEconomy`
- `#NewSpace`

### Engagement Best Practices
- Keep tweets under 250 characters when possible (better engagement)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include a link to SpaceNexus when relevant
- Reply to comments within 2 hours
- Quote-tweet industry news with SpaceNexus context
- Pin the most recent feature announcement or milestone tweet
