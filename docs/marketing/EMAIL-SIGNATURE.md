## Usage Notes

- The HTML version uses inline styles for maximum email client compatibility
- The indigo accent color (#6366f1) matches the SpaceNexus brand palette
- The "Free to start" CTA button links directly to the registration page
- Works in Gmail, Outlook (desktop and web), Apple Mail, and Thunderbird
- No external images required -- pure HTML/CSS for fast loading and no broken images
