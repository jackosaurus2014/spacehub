## Link-Worthy Content to Create

Content assets specifically designed to attract backlinks:

### 1. Annual Space Economy Report
- "State of the Space Economy 2026" — Comprehensive data report
- Include original charts, data tables, market size estimates
- Offer embeddable chart widgets for other sites
- Target: Industry publications and blogs will cite the data

### 2. Space Company Database Widget
- Embeddable widget showing top space companies by market cap
- Other sites can embed it → links back to SpaceNexus
- Target: Space blogs, investment sites, news outlets

### 3. Launch Cost Calculator
- Standalone interactive tool at /mission-cost
- "Calculate your satellite launch cost" — highly linkable utility
- Target: Space startup blogs, educational sites

### 4. Space Industry Salary Data
- "Space Industry Salaries 2026" — Survey-based content
- Engineers, analysts, program managers, executives
- Target: Career sites, university programs, HR publications

### 5. Interactive Satellite Count Tracker
- Real-time count of objects in orbit with historical trend
- Embed code for other sites
- Target: Science education sites, media outlets

## Tracking & Measurement

Track backlink acquisition:
- **Google Search Console** → Links report (after GSC registration)
- **Ahrefs/Semrush** → Domain Rating, referring domains (optional paid tool)
- **Manual tracking** → Spreadsheet of submissions, dates, status

Target metrics:
- 10 directory listings in first month
- 2-3 guest posts per quarter
- 1 podcast appearance per quarter
- 20+ referring domains within 6 months
