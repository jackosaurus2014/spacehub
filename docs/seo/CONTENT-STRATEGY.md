# SpaceNexus Content Strategy for SEO

**Date**: February 18, 2026
**Goal**: Drive organic traffic through high-value content targeting space industry keywords

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. Space Economy Analysis (Transactional/Informational)
Target audience: Investors, analysts, executives
Keywords: space market size, space stocks, space company valuation

**Content Ideas:**
- Monthly "Space Economy Snapshot" blog post (recurring)
- "Space IPO Tracker: Who's Going Public in 2026?"
- "Space Industry Revenue Breakdown by Segment"
- "How to Evaluate a Space Startup for Investment"
- "Space SPAC Performance: Lessons from 2021-2025"

### 2. Technical Guides (Educational)
Target audience: Engineers, program managers, students
Keywords: how to track satellites, orbital mechanics, launch costs

**Content Ideas:**
- "Beginner's Guide to Orbital Mechanics" (pillar page)
- "Understanding TLE Data for Satellite Tracking"
- "Spectrum Management 101 for Satellite Operators"
- "Space Insurance: What You Need to Know"
- "ITAR/EAR Compliance Checklist for Space Startups"

### 3. Industry Reports (Link-worthy)
Target audience: Media, researchers, industry insiders
Keywords: space industry report, space economy data, launch statistics

**Content Ideas:**
- "Annual Space Launch Statistics Report" (recurring)
- "Space Startup Funding: Year in Review"
- "Government Space Contracts: Annual Analysis"
- "Satellite Constellation Growth Tracker"
- "Space Workforce Demographics Report"

### 4. Comparison & Tool Content (Bottom-funnel)
Target audience: Prospective users evaluating tools
Keywords: SpaceNexus vs, space data platform, satellite tracking tool

**Content Ideas:**
- More /compare pages (vs. Planet Labs, vs. Privateer Space, vs. LeoLabs)
- "Best Space Industry Tools for [Role]" series
- "SpaceNexus Feature Deep Dives" series
- Tool-specific landing pages (/mission-cost, /satellites as entry points)

### 5. City/Regional Pages (Local SEO)
Target audience: Space professionals in specific regions
Keywords: space companies in [city], aerospace jobs [location]

**Existing:** Houston, Washington DC, LA, Denver, Seattle, San Francisco, Cape Canaveral, Huntsville

**Expansion Ideas:**
- International: Toulouse (Airbus/ESA), Bangalore (ISRO), Tokyo (JAXA), Bremen (OHB/Airbus DS)
- More US: Tucson (Raytheon), El Segundo (Aerospace Corp), Colorado Springs (Space Force)
