# Why SpaceNexus? The Space Industry Intelligence Platform You've Been Waiting For

*First published on LinkedIn — SpaceNexus LLC*

---

The commercial space industry is booming. With over $546 billion flowing through the global space economy and projections hitting $1.1 trillion by 2030, there's never been a more exciting time to build, invest in, or work in space.

But here's the problem: **the intelligence infrastructure hasn't kept up.**

Large defense contractors have access to Bloomberg terminals, expensive consulting reports from Bryce Tech and Euroconsult, and dedicated market intelligence teams. The rest of us — the startup founders, the angel investors, the engineers exploring career moves, the procurement officers evaluating vendors — we've been left to piece together insights from scattered newsletters, outdated government databases, and word of mouth.

**That's why we built SpaceNexus.**

## What Is SpaceNexus?

SpaceNexus is a comprehensive space industry intelligence platform that brings together the data, tools, and community that space professionals need — all in one place. Think of it as the Bloomberg Terminal for the space industry, but accessible to everyone.

Here's what you'll find:

### Market Intelligence
- **100+ company profiles** with financial data, satellite assets, facility locations, and competitive analysis
- **Funding rounds database** tracking $15B+ in annual space investment across 150+ deals
- **M&A tracker** monitoring industry consolidation trends
- **Market segment analysis** breaking down the $546B space economy into 12 investable segments
- **Space Score** ratings evaluating company health and growth potential

### Professional Tools
- **Mission Simulator** — Plan missions with real launch vehicle data, delta-V calculations, and cost estimates
- **Orbital Mechanics Calculator** — Hohmann transfers, launch windows, and orbital parameters
- **Thermal Analysis Calculator** — Equilibrium temperatures, material presets, and power balance
- **Radiation Environment Calculator** — Dose estimates for any orbit with shielding analysis
- **Link Budget & Power Budget Calculators** — Essential satellite design tools
- **Constellation Designer** — Design multi-plane satellite constellations
- **Launch Cost Calculator** — Compare $/kg across 9+ launch vehicles

### Business Resources

## Why Now?

Three trends are converging that make SpaceNexus essential:

1. **The democratization of space access.** Launch costs have dropped from $54,500/kg (Space Shuttle) to under $1,500/kg (Falcon 9 reusable). Starship aims for $10-50/kg. This means more companies, more missions, and more professionals who need intelligence tools.

2. **The explosion of commercial space.** Over 10,500 active satellites are in orbit. Mega-constellations from Starlink, OneWeb, and Kuiper are reshaping telecommunications. Space tourism, in-orbit manufacturing, and lunar commerce are moving from science fiction to business plans.

3. **The intelligence gap.** While the market has grown 10x in two decades, the intelligence tools available to most participants haven't changed. SpaceNexus fills that gap with modern, accessible, and comprehensive data.
