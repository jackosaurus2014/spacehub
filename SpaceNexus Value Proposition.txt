WHAT SPACENEXUS OFFERS THAT NOBODY ELSE DOES
---------------------------------------------

SpaceNexus is the first and only platform that brings together real-time space
industry data, interactive tools, regulatory intelligence, and market analytics
in a single, affordable, self-service application. Today, professionals are
forced to piece together insights from dozens of disconnected sources:

  - NEWS OUTLETS (SpaceNews, Via Satellite, Payload Space) publish articles and
    commentary but offer no structured data, no interactive tools, and no way to
    analyze trends or compare options. You read their stories; you can't act on
    their data because there is none.

  - PREMIUM CONSULTING FIRMS (Euroconsult, Quilty Space, Northern Sky Research)
    produce excellent analysis -- at $3,000 to $10,000+ per report, published
    annually. By the time you read it, the data is already months old. These

    professionals.

  - GOVERNMENT TOOLS (LeoLabs, ExoAnalytic, Slingshot Aerospace) focus narrowly
    on space domain awareness for military and government clients. They don't
    serve the broader business community.

  - ENGINEERING SOFTWARE (AGI/STK, GMAT) is built for orbital mechanics and
    mission design -- powerful, but irrelevant for business strategy, legal
    compliance, or market intelligence.

SpaceNexus fills the gap between all four. It is a DATA PLATFORM, not a news
site. It is REAL-TIME, not annual. It is INTERACTIVE, not static. And it is
AFFORDABLE, not enterprise-only.
